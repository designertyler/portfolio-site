Introduction
What Partner Center does
Partner Center is your onboarding workspace for launching and managing services in the catalog. It guides your team through required setup tasks, captures product and support metadata, and tracks progress across approvals so nothing is missed before release.
During onboarding, you will complete checklist items, assign owners, and submit lifecycle approvals from one place. As your service matures, Partner Center continues to support updates like feature launches, pricing changes, new plans, and deprecation workflows with clear governance at every step.
